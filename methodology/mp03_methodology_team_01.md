
Methodoloogy Section:

Our team used SEC filing analysis pipeline to analyze the geographical operations that occured in the Financial Services and Travel and Hospitality industries. We modified the ticker list to better and more reliabily retrieve candidates. The main goal of the project was to build an integrated geographic map showing how these two industries differ in physical expansion, restructuring, and operational activity.

For the Financial Services pipeline, we used a mix of banks, payment processors, insurance firms, and asset management companies because they frequently report branch closures, office relocations, and restructuring activities. For the Travel and Hospitality pipeline, we used hotel chains, airlines, cruise operators, and online travel companies because they commonly report new hotel openings, route expansions, and resort developments.
